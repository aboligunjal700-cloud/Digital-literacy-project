# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails
- Always verify website URLs before entering information
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone

## UPI / Financial Safety
- Do not share UPI PIN with anyone
- Verify payment requests before accepting
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Helpline: 1930
